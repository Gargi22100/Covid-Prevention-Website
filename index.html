how to prevent virus

wash your place

Keep your surroundings clean. Wash your place and maintain a good hygiene.Stay safe and healthy.

maintain distance

Please maintain a social distance while talking to others even if they are your relavtives.It is very neccessary to maintain distance.

don't touch face

It is advisable not to touch your face very enough.You must first wash your hands before touching your face.

wash your hand

Washing your hands is a must to stay safe from this deadly pandemic.We must keep washing our hands in short intervals so that the virus does not spread.

use napkin

You must use a napkin while sneezing or coughing.It is a good habit to use tissue or napkin in public places to avoid spread of virus.

wear a mask

It is complusory to wear a mask whenever you go out to meet someone or to purchase your essentials.Without mask you must not even step out of your house.
